Fridge App

Fridge App is a full-stack android app which is used to help manage all things fridge related and minimise food waste.

Users scan products into the system and scan them out once they have been used up. The app then sorts products into their different categories and allows you to view your fridge inventory from your phone.

Notifications and an in-app inbox also notify you of food that you need to use up quickly to prevent expiry.

The app also automatically adds items to a shopping list when they're scanned out and this shopping list can then be manually customised.

App users can join the same fridge with an account system.

Download Version 1.0.2 (Android APK)

User Features:

  • Tracking your fridge inventory
  • Notifications and inbox for food expiry
  • Automatically generated shopping list
  • Join a fridge with other users

Technical Features:

  • React Native
  • Firebase
  • JWT
  • Express.js
